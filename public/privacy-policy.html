Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

Precision Appraisal Zone (“PAZ”, “we”, “us”) provides independent auto appraisal services including Diminished Value (DV), Loss of Use (LoU), and Total Loss valuation support. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details — name, email, phone, communication preferences.
  • Claim & vehicle data — VIN, estimates, repair invoices, photos, police reports, insurer offers, and related correspondence you or your representatives provide.
  • Technical data — IP address, device/browser, and pages viewed for security and basic analytics.

How We Use Information

  • Evaluate cases and prepare appraisal reports (DV/LoU/Total Loss).
  • Communicate with you and your authorized representatives (e.g., attorneys, public adjusters, body shops).
  • Improve services, site security, and customer support.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud/abuse.

Sharing

We share information only as needed to deliver services (for example, with your attorney, public adjuster, or repair facility) or as required by law. We do not sell personal information.

Retention

We retain records for a commercially reasonable period to support your claim and comply with legal obligations, then securely delete or anonymize them.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the sensitivity of data. No system is 100% secure.

Your Choices

  • Access/Correction: Contact us to review or update your information.
  • Deletion: Request deletion subject to legal/contractual obligations.
  • Marketing: You can opt out of non-essential communications.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 13.

Jurisdiction

Florida law governs this policy, except where preempted by U.S. federal law.
